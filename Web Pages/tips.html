Useful Tips For GATE


Plan for Success at Exam

  1. Aim for taking Gate exam varies from candidate to candidate. If students aim to get into IIt or equivalent institutions, very high percentile is required.
  2. Normally 3 to 4 months of serious study should be sufficient for preparation.
  3. In the first run, study all the topics as per syllabus.
  4. In the revision stage, concentrate more on selected topics.
  5. Try to solve papers papers of previous years. This will give you an idea about the mismatch between the actual paper and your level of preparation.
  6. Fill up the gap by extra study.
  7. Try to take up practice tests.

Topper's View

  1. One day before the exam, revise the course.
  2. During exam, try section A first. Q1 carry 1 mark each and Q2 carry 2 marks each.
  3. Manage the time well during the exam.
  4. In the hall, one should not get panicky upon recieving the question paper.
    Time management is important but not at the cost of answering the paper accurately.
  5. Even you find the paper tough, DO NOT panic. It will be hard for all.
  6. Do not try to get hold of the entire syllabus prescribed for Gate. A selective preparation would be a very helpful concept and should be learned accurately.
    Memorization of any problem without a clear idea of the concept will be a futile exercise.
  7. I feel that it is better to have flexible time plan before going to exam and have a balance of mind. It helps in avoiding mistakes.
